User Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the Setora app. Follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. 1 Open the Setora app and go to the Profile tab.
  2. 2 Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner.
  3. 3 Select Account.
  4. 4 Tap Delete Account and confirm.
