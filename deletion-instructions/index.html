Account

Delete your Setora account directly in the app.

You can remove your Setora account and associated user data from within the app settings. Follow the steps below to complete the deletion process.

Steps to delete your account

  1. 1
    Open the Setora app and go to the Profile tab.
  2. 2
    Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner.
  3. 3
    Select Account.
  4. 4
    Tap Delete Account and confirm the action.
Need help?

If you run into issues, contact patrick.mihalcea@gmail.com.

Before you delete

Deleting your account removes access to your stored workout and profile data associated with that account.
