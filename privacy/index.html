Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Setora may collect, how it is used to operate the app, and how to contact us about privacy or data deletion requests.

Last updated: April 28, 2026
What Setora is

Setora is a workout tracking app for routines, workout history, analytics, progression, and body metrics.

How to contact us

Email patrick.mihalcea@gmail.com for privacy or data requests.

Deletion requests

Account deletion can be initiated inside the app or reviewed on the Data Deletion page.

Information We Collect

We may collect your email address, login email from Google or Facebook, height, weight, birthday, uploaded photos, workout data, goals, progress metrics, and any other information you choose to enter into the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to create and manage your account, store workout history, track progress, personalize your experience, provide app functionality, maintain security, and respond to support requests.

Authentication

If you sign in using Google or Facebook, we may receive basic account information such as your email address and account identifier for authentication purposes.

Data Storage

Setora uses Supabase for backend services and Cloudflare R2 for file storage, including uploaded images or media. Your data may be processed and stored by these service providers as needed to operate the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only as necessary to operate, secure, and maintain Setora.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information while your account is active or as needed to provide the app. You may request account deletion or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email listed on this page.

Children's Privacy

Setora is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Questions or requests

Contact patrick.mihalcea@gmail.com if you have privacy questions or need help with deletion.

Related pages

Visit Support for general help or Data Deletion for the in-app deletion steps.
