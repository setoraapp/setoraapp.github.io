Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Setora is a workout tracking app that helps users manage routines, log workouts, and track fitness progress.

Information We Collect

We may collect your email address, login email from Google or Facebook, height, weight, birthday, uploaded photos, workout data, goals, progress metrics, and other information you choose to enter into the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to create and manage your account, save your workout history, track progress, personalize your experience, provide app features, maintain security, and respond to support requests.

Authentication

If you sign in using Google or Facebook, we may receive basic account information such as your email address and account identifier for authentication purposes.

Data Storage

Setora uses Supabase for backend services and Cloudflare R2 for file storage, including uploaded images or media. Your data may be processed and stored by these service providers as needed to operate the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only as necessary to operate, secure, and maintain Setora.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information while your account is active or as needed to provide the app. You may request account deletion or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Setora is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
