Founder story

Built from a real training journey.

Setora started from the same frustration a lot of committed lifters run into: most fitness apps do one thing well, but almost none of them bring planning, execution, progression, and insight into one system that feels serious to use.

The goal was to build something more complete and more thoughtful. A product that helps people train with structure, understand what is changing, and keep improving without bouncing between five different tools.

That is the standard Setora is built around: clear design, capable tracking, and training support that respects the user’s intent to get better over time.
